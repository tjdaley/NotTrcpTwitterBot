Analyze My Tweets!

AnalyzeMyTweets.com is the companion to the AnalyzeMyTweets Twitter bot. If you follow @analyzemytweets on Twitter, the bot will start tracking your follower population, analyzing your tweets, and sending you reports when things change. The reports are sent as DMs to your Twitter account. When you receive a DM from @analyzemytweets, it will contain a unique link to this web site allowing you to view a more detailed report.
